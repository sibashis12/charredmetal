BlitzQuiz
, the best website for quiz enthusiasts! Whether you're a lore hound, a quiz master, or just looking to have some fun, we've got some quiz rounds for you. Our dynamic quizzes will keep you entertained as you try to learn new trivia, to beat the high score or to just relax after a stressful day. Powered by Trivia API.

We have 2 modes as of now:
  • Classic Mode- The classic mode has 20 questions with 4 options each, and one correct answer. You will be provided with 10 minutes for the quiz. Currently a work in progress, please use Bullet Node in the time being.
    You can navigate freely between the questions.
  • Bullet Mode- The bullet mode has 20 questions with 4 options each, and one correct answer. The questions will be rapid-fire, and you will be provided with 15 seconds for each question.

    See Rules->

    You will have one lifeline which you can use by not answering the question. If it is used, score will turn blue. If your option is incorrect, score will turn red, and if your option is correct, score will turn green. The score will remain white if you do not have any lifelines and do not answer the question.


    Back navigation is not allowed.
